Stirfry Spaghetti

This spaghetti is flavorful, rich in protein and perfect for family dinners

Recipe Information

Preparation time:30 minutes

Cooking Time:45 minutes

Servings:6 people

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Groundnut oil
  • 1 Large finely chopped onions
  • Tomato Paste
  • Pepper mix roughly blended:Tomato,Scotch Bonnet pepper,Red bell pepper
  • Yellow bell pepper
  • Green bell pepper
  • Soy sauce
  • Eggs
  • Sausage
  • Chopped Beef
  • Salt and seasoning to taste

Instructions

  1. Per boil spaghetti for 12 minutes,sieve and set aside
  2. Season beef,boil and set aside
  3. Heat groundnut oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  4. Add chopped onions,saute until softened
  5. Add tomaato paste and leave to fry
  6. Add the roughly blended mix,salt,seasoning and leave to fry
  7. Add yellow and green scotch bonnet pepper and leave to cook
  8. Fry eggs and set aside
  9. Fry sausages and set aside
  10. Pour spaghetti into fried pepper mixture and mix together properly
  11. Add soy sauce
  12. Leave to cook under low heat for 3 minutes
  13. Add fried eggs,sausages and chopped beef
  14. mix everything togeher
  15. Serve spaghetti with any protein of your choice

Tips

For a richer flavor,you can add more proteins to your stirfry
You can also double the sauce and freeze for later
